India's Talent Dhamaal 2016

We at Rich Looks Make-up Studio, presents ourselves, as media production house, after having a huge experience in the industry. We have been at the cutting edge of the media production and fashion world for last 20 years and always produce precisely what is required to make a show expressive, entertaining and influential, and an occurrence to creates all around entertainment. With a huge experience as a bollywood make-up artist, Mr. Gulshan Idrish, who is the director of this company, has already done ranges of successful fashion show / makeup award shows and now he is representing the talent show on a big screen of Indian premier entertainment TV channel. The aim of the show is to explore the talent that is hidden inside the talented people and produce exceptionally talented singers, dancers, and actor / actress and makeup artists to the bollywood industry.
